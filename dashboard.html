How are settlements tracked?
Settlements record outstanding balances between you and others. 'Lent' means they owe you, and 'Borrowed' means you owe them. Mark as settled when paid.
Why use custom tags?
Tags help categorize your spendings (e.g., Food, Travel, Rent). This powers the tag breakdown chart on the dashboard, making it easy to identify saving opportunities.
