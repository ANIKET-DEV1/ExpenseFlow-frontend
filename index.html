Frequently asked questions

Is my financial data secure?
Yes. Your data is stored on a private server and accessible only through your authenticated session. We use secure HTTP-only cookies so your session token is never exposed to JavaScript or third-party scripts.
How do I add an expense?
After logging in, go to Expenses and click "Add Expense". Fill in the amount, pick a tag, choose the payment type (Cash, UPI, or Card), and set the date. That's it — takes under 10 seconds.
What are tags, and do I need them?
Tags are categories you create yourself — like "Groceries", "Rent", or "Petrol". Every expense needs one tag. You can create unlimited tags for free.
Can I track money I owe or money owed to me?
Yes — that's what Settlements are for. Add a record with the person's name, amount, and direction. Mark it paid when done. You can edit amounts and status anytime.
Can I see my spending month by month?
Yes. On the Expenses page, switch to "By Month" view to see expenses grouped by month with totals. Switch to "By Tag" to see per-category breakdowns.
Is this free to use?
Yes, completely free. No subscription, no credit card required. Register and start using it.
